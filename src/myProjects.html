Multilocation project

Multilocation Car Dealership Management System, developed in C# WPF and integrated with SQL Server, is a robust solution for efficiently handling rental information across a diverse fleet of vehicles.

Its user-friendly interface ensures a seamless experience, allowing users to navigate effortlessly and access key details such as vehicle information, rental dates, and customer details.
Advanced search capabilities enable quick location of specific rentals through ID or customer name.

The dynamic redisplay feature provides real-time fleet updates with a single click, ensuring users have access to the latest information.
The 'Add New Rental' section simplifies fleet expansion, enhancing adaptability and scalability. Multilocation sets a new standard for car dealership management systems with its comprehensive and efficient solution.

It optimizes workflows within a sleek design, ultimately boosting overall productivity in the management of rental information for car dealerships.

Android project

Introducing an engaging and interactive experience, our Tic Tac Toe app redefines classic gaming by seamlessly blending the timeless appeal of the game with modern functionalities.

Developed on Android Studio, the application incorporates a unique twist by integrating a dynamic user list. Players can either select their names from an existing list or personalize the experience by adding their names to the roster.

This innovative approach fosters a sense of identity and ownership, elevating the gaming experience. As users engage in exhilarating rounds of Tic Tac Toe, the app intelligently saves and recalls scores, ensuring that each player's achievements persist across sessions.

The intuitive design promotes accessibility, making it easy for users to dive into the game and continue their journey, seamlessly transitioning from one match to the next with their accumulated scores intact.

With a thoughtful combination of nostalgia and contemporary features, our Tic Tac Toe app promises not only a delightful gaming experience but also a sense of community as players carve their names into the evolving narrative of strategic triumphs.
